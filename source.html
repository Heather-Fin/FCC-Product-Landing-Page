WHAT IS CG GAMES?

CG Games Prelims is more than a competition, it's an experience! In one of the most unique fitness events in the nation, your endurance, strength, agility and speed will be tested.

HOW IT WORKS

Join thousands of CG Campers and Athletes of all fitness levels to compete in one of the most unique fitness events in the nation! Held on Sat, July 20 & Sat, July 27 in select cities nationwide, you can take on the competition as an individual or on a team of four. The top teams and individual Athletes from each division will qualify to face the best-of-the-best at CG Games Finals in Austin, TX on Sat, Nov 9.

DIVISIONS

One competition, two ways to participate! Challenge yourself and compete as an individual or grab some friends and build a team for extra accountability! Athletes must register under the division based on their age on or before July 20, 2019.
